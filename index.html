About

The Random Password Generator project was inspired by the need for a simple, yet secure way to generate passwords for various applications. It was born out of a desire to create a tool that could help individuals and businesses protect their online accounts and sensitive information.

Less is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, this project represents our dedication to learning and applying new skills in web development. We embarked on this journey with the goal of creating something meaningful and useful, and we are proud of what we have accomplished.

For more information about our journey and the development process, please visit our Portfolio Project page.
